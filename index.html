Having worked in the art and press research industry for over twenty years I decided to reskill to pursue a more sustainable and consistent career.

I have always had a keen interested in software development so I decided to focus my efforts in this industry.

As software technology is continuously changing I am committed to keeping up to date with current trends and styles. I acquired a certificate in Software Development on Udemy with The App Brewery in 2023 but I also spend a huge amount of time training myself on new skills to ensure I am constantly developing.

I have also used my role as a voluntary coder with the Scottish Tech Army as an opportunity to hone and practice my learned skills. This was a great opportunity to develop a website, something I would be confident and grateful to have the opportunity to do professionally.

I continuously enhance my knowledge with up-to-date fullstack technologies, I have a natural interest in both front & back end development. I have a solid understanding of many web markup languages, including HTML5, CSS3/Sass and ReactJS (both hooks and classes). I also have a solid understanding of server-side PHP, Javascript/ES6 and NextJS. I have a good understanding of cross-browser compatibility and asynchronous request handling. In addition, I have developed software apps in C#.
